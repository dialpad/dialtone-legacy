# Usage

Use d-{fs|headline}{n} to change an element's font-size.

.d-fs14

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

.d-fs18

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

.d-fs24

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

.d-fs32

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

.d-fs42

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

.d-headline36

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

.d-headline48

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

.d-headline54

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

<p class="d-fs14">...</p>
<p class="d-fs18">...</p>
<p class="d-fs24">...</p>
<p class="d-fs42">...</p>
<p class="d-headline36 d-lh1">...</p>
<p class="d-headline48 d-lh1">...</p>
<p class="d-headline54 d-lh1">...</p>
