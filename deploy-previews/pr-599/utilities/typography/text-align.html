Text Align

Utilities for controlling an element's text alignment.

# Classes

ClassOutput
.d-ta-lefttext-align: left !important;
.d-ta-righttext-align: right !important;
.d-ta-centertext-align: center !important;
.d-ta-justifytext-align: justify !important;
.d-ta-unsettext-align: unset !important;

# Usage

Use d-ta-{n} to change an element's text alignment.

.d-ta-left

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

.d-ta-center

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

.d-ta-right

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

.d-ta-justify

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. This needs a width applied to it to work.

.d-ta-unset

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

<p class="d-ta-left">...</p>
<p class="d-ta-center">...</p>
<p class="d-ta-right">...</p>
<p class="d-ta-justify d-w332">...</p>
<p class="d-ta-unset">...</p>
