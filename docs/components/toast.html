--- layout: page title: Toast description: Toast notices, sometimes called snackbars, are time-based messages that appear based on the user or other users actions. They contain at-a-glance information about outcomes. Sometimes they are paired with actions. ---

Breakdown

Toast messages have a content area, icon, message, and possibly metadata. They are placed within a wrapper.
